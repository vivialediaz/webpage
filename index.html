About

I am Associate Professor at the Department of Mathematics, Universidad Nacional del Sur, and researcher at INMABB. I received my PhD in Mathematics in 2008 (UNS).

My research focuses on geometric mechanics, nonholonomic systems, reduction theory, variational calculus, and structure-preserving machine learning.

Research Areas

  • Geometric Mechanics
  • Nonholonomic Systems & Symmetry Reduction
  • Variational Methods & Griffiths Formalism
  • Lagrangian and Hamiltonian Neural Networks
  • Structure-Preserving Machine Learning
  • Geometric Control & Lyapunov Methods
